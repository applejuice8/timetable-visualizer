Enrolment

Please take note of the following important information regarding online course enrolment for the April 2026 semester:

1. iZone Online Course Enrolment Period
The iZone online course enrolment will open on Tuesday, 14 April 2026 at 12:00 PM and close on Sunday, 19 April 2026 at 11:59 PM.

2. System Downtime
The iZone online course enrolment system will be unavailable daily during the following times due to system updates. Kindly plan your enrolment activities accordingly.
o 12:10 AM – 1:10 AM
o 2:10 PM – 3:10 PM

3. Enrolment Guide
Please refer to the Course Enrolment Guide for detailed instructions on how to perform online subject enrolment via iZone.

4. Teaching and Learning Mode
You are advised to consult your subject lecturers and check your respective eLearn course sites for more information on the teaching and learning delivery modes.

5. Group Enrolment
Please ensure that you enrol in the lecture, tutorial, practical, and workshop groups assigned to your programme and intake. The School reserves the right to reject or amend any enrolment if it does not comply with the official timetable.

For the courses IST1014 (Introduction to Statistics), IST2024 (Applied Statistics), and STAT1114/STAT2044 (Statistics for Data Analytics), students must enrol in the same group for both Practical and Tutorial classes.

6. Elective Courses
For guidance on selecting field/discipline elective courses, please refer to the “Field Elective” folder located on the left-side panel of the SCAI eLearn site.

7. Free Elective Courses
Enrolment for Free Elective courses will be made available from Thursday, 16 April 2026, 12:00 PM until Sunday, 19 April 2026, 11:59 PM. To view the Free Electives courses, please go to Xplorer Hub page at https://sunwayuniversity.edu.my/courses/xplorer-hub

8. Course Visibility in iZone
If you are unable to view a course in iZone, please check whether you have fulfilled the required pre-requisite(s).
